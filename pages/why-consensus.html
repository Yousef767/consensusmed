Why Consensus Medical system ?
    Features
  • Consensus Medical Systems established in 1995 is a leader of Cardiovascular structured reporting software. Thousands of Vascular and Echo studies are reported each day by Consensus user friendly amazing reporting and outcome analysis software applications VascuPro™, EkoPro™ and VascuBase™. Consensus applications has withstood the test of time and continue to enhance the clinical productivity of many reputable institutes worldwide.
